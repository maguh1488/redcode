        ========================
         ==== Life Project ====
        ========================

The "Development" folder houses profiles
that are in the process of being made or
undergoing maintenance. 

The use of such profiles is not supported
in any meaning of the word. The finished
product may not be compatible with what is
currently in the Development folder, making
it dangerous to use them with normal leveling.

If you wish to test them and understand what
is a profile issue or a bot issue, and the 
risks feel free to go ahead. Issue reporting
is always encouraged. I will try to report 
bugs to the Trello page, please go there 
before trying to submit any bug.

https://trello.com/b/QuyDxb43/life-project
